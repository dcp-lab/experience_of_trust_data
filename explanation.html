Surveys

You will first complete three surveys: These surveys will help us to understand your values, current emotional and anxiety states.

Game Instructions

After completing the surveys, you will participate in a game with online participants.
At each new trial, a new participant will be playing with you. Each new participant will get access the survey on values you have completed.
The rules of the game will be explained to you after the completion of the surveys.
At the end of the experiment, you will be asked to complete the two surveys on your current emotional and anxiety states you answered earlier.
